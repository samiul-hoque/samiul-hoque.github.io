About Me

I am Samiul Hoque! Welcome to my homepage. I am a Maker, DIY enthusiast and an electronics nerd. I like making things in general, ranging from wooden furniture to sophisticated payloads designed to reach beyond the ozone layer.

I am an active member of the local maker community and a strong believer of the Open Source Hardware Movement. I believe maker literacy should be a part of our basic education system. Right now I mostly work as a freelance Instructor for local Fab Labs.

I graduated from Fab Academy on the 2019 cycle. Fab Academy is an intense Digital Fabrication course run by the Center for Bits & Atoms, MIT. It is run simultaneously over 70 labs all across the globe in a distributed class model.

Feel free to have a look at my Fab Academy profile.
