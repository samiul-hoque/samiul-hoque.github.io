TurtleSense

I first got involved with Creative Conservation Alliance through Fahim Zaman, a University Senior from my days at BRAC University. He's an eccentric College dropout like me who's a co-founder of Nature Aquatics. He's an amazing photographer as well and currently he's working for CCA at their Wild Tortoise Preservation Center at Bhawal Reserve Forest. A few years back, I helped them with a Turtle Egg incubator and guess what, those hatchlings are now saving an endangered species.

I was back there recently to survey the place to set up a sensor mesh for monitoring environmental conditions at the reserve. My biggest concern was power, assuming the place was in the middle of a forest, I'd have to go with solar power. But it turns out, they have a quite nice facility running with 24x7 power and backup generators as well.

Right now the project prospect looks like I'll be working with 2.4Ghz and my favourite microcontroller family, the ESP8266 and ESP32. And I have a nice little nano pi lying around at home which can run node-red well enough for atleast a few dozens of these sensors. I'll keep posting here for updates regarding the technical details
